Go to Google!

Google Maps for mobile has a view camera. You see the map through the camera. This plugin provides animateCamera() and moveCamera() methods. The animateCamera() moves the camera with animation, while the other hand without animation. Default animation time of animateCamera() method is 4 seconds.
  • moveCamera()
  • animateCamera()

with jQuery

var GOOGLE = new plugin.google.maps.LatLng(37.422858, -122.085065); map.setDiv($("#map_canvas")[0]); map.moveCamera({ 'target': GOOGLE, 'zoom': 0 }); map.animateCamera({ 'target': GOOGLE, 'tilt': 60, 'zoom': 18, 'bearing': 140, 'duration': 10000 });
